Facing Criminal Allegations?

Everything is at stake: your family, your finances, your very freedom. Indeed, your reputation and perhaps your employment are now in jeopardy. All because of one bad decision you might’ve made, or because of one wrongful accusation. Does the idea of serving jail time – or worse, prison – sound scary? Well, it is.

Don’t Go it Alone!

Imagine your worst outcome. Now, don’t let it happen; don’t attempt to resolve the situation on your own! Just as you wouldn’t attempt to fix a cavity or remove your appendix without a qualified expert, don’t attempt to resolve your legal issues without an attorney.

Let Us Protect You.

With hundreds of years of cumulative experience among our attorneys, there’s nothing we haven’t seen, no problem we can’t fix. Our firm has dealt with thousands of criminal cases. Tap into our expertise so you’re well-equipped to handle whatever the system throws at you.

Let’s Do This.

Give us a call. We can defend you! Whether you’ve actually been arrested or just handed a summons, know that you’re now involved in a fight that may have permanent, lasting, repercussions if not confronted appropriately and swiftly. Whether you want it or not, you’re involved in a legal battle that will likely determine your future. Now, let’s work to find a way to minimize the damage.

Felony? We’ve Got Your Back.

When faced with serious criminal allegations, it’s vital to have an experienced and trusted lawyer at your side. Our criminal-defense attorneys – all recognized experts – cooperate and coordinate with one another to provide the best guidance, legal advice, protection of your rights, and overall dedicated legal defense – both in and out of Court.

Misdemeanor? No Problem.

We offer clients the utmost discretion when handling their criminal matters. Our attorneys understand that accidents happen, and mistakes are made. A handful of bad decisions do not define who you are, nor should they define your future. Nevertheless, you need protection, as well as an opportunity to make reparations, without being handed an unjust sentence at the hand of an unsympathetic or crusading prosecutor.

Our Commitment to You.

At our firm we’re fond of saying that the most important case we have is "yours." It’s that simple. After an initial consultation, we will return your calls promptly, answer your questions accurately, and work arduously on your behalf to successfully resolve your legal issues.
