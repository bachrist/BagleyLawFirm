Facing a Divorce?

Everything is at stake. Your children, your finances, your lifestyle. The things you have worked so hard to obtain are up for grabs. Does a lifetime of alimony or weekend visitations with your kids sound scary?

Don’t Go it Alone!

Imagine your best outcome. It may be a clean break, a fair deal, or something in between. Although it might feel out of reach, if you do what’s necessary, you can still retire how you always dreamed, be the parent you always strived to be, and find a way to rebuild your life and find happiness.

Let Us Protect You

With hundreds of years of cumulative experience among our attorneys, there’s nothing we haven’t seen. Our firm has dealt with thousands of divorce and child custody cases. Tap into our expertise so you’re well-equipped to handle whatever life throws at you over the coming months.

Let’s Do This.

Give us a call. If legal papers have been served, whether you want it or not, you’re involved in a fight. Don't make the mistake of waiting. And if you are in the unfortunate position of having to be the person to file for divorce, then use this time to get the advantage. If you know it's unavoidable, whatever the circumstances, act now.

Divorce? Deal with it Head On

We understand the sensitive and all-too-often awkward circumstances that bring our clients to us; the breakdown of a family is hard for everyone involved. In fact, it may be the most painful experience you ever face. But from us there’s no judgment, just empathy and expertise. Rest assured, there’s light at the end of the tunnel.

Child Custody? Your Kids Need You.

Divorce and child custody cases are especially hard on kids. Don’t let your soon-to-be-ex put them in the middle; your children aren’t pawns. There’s nothing our attorneys pride themselves on more than keeping kids safe through legal proceedings and beyond.
